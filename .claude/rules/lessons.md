## Learning log

When you encounter any of the following during a session, immediately append a new `## heading` + explanation to `docs/LESSONS.md`:

- A bug caused by your own change (root cause + why you got it wrong)
- A review cycle finding that turned out to be a false positive in practice
- Runtime behavior of Teams/Chrome that differs from what static analysis predicts
- A design decision that was questioned but confirmed to be correct (and why)

Do not batch these — write each entry as soon as the learning occurs.
